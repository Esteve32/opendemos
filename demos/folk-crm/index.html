Team Onboarding & Adoption

A tool is only effective if it's used consistently. Follow this simple timeline to ensure team-wide adoption and build the right habits from day one.

30-Minute Kickoff

Demonstrate the core workflow: Add contact from LinkedIn, add a note, and link to the Notion page.

Establish a "Folk First" Rule

Mandate that all new contact info and interaction notes are logged in Folk to prevent data silos.

Review Weekly

For the first month, spend 5 minutes in your team meeting sharing a useful interaction logged in Folk. This reinforces the habit.
